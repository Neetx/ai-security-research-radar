## New trend: adversarial-trigger-backdoor-004

Four independent author groups across three model domains (diffusion models ×2,
sensor/IoT classifiers, federated learning) all published the same week on adversarial
trigger implantation — backdoors that create persistent hidden behaviors invisible to
clean-distribution evaluation. Common structure: a hidden trigger activates malicious
behavior; the implant survives training with imperceptibly low poison rates. All 4
papers submitted 2026-06-22/24. Seeded at `medium` confidence, `seed` stage; watching
for evidence in safety-critical deployed systems or a 5th group tightening the
threat model toward a dominant domain.
